Step 1 of 3 — Create a free Plaid account
Plaid is the industry-standard bank connector. Signing up is free, instant, and requires no credit card.
1

Sign up for a free Plaid Developer account

Plaid's Developer account is completely free for personal use and gives you access to connect up to 10 real bank accounts — more than enough to track your full financial picture. No credit card required.

Open dashboard.plaid.com/signup ↗
2

Fill in the sign-up form

Enter your name, email, and a password. When asked what you're building, choose Personal Finance or any option — it doesn't affect functionality.

3

Verify your email

Plaid will send you a confirmation link. Click it, then log in to the Plaid Dashboard.

4

You're in the Plaid Dashboard

Once you see the Plaid Dashboard, you're ready for the next step — getting your API keys.

Step 2 of 3 — Get your API keys
Your API keys tell this app how to talk to Plaid on your behalf. They stay on your machine only.
1

Go to Team Settings → Keys

In the Plaid Dashboard, click your account name (top-left) → Team Settings → Keys.

Open Plaid Keys page ↗
2

Copy your Client ID

You'll see a client_id field at the top. Copy that value — it's the same for all environments.

3

Copy your Production Secret

For real bank accounts, copy the Production secret. If you want to test with fake data first, use the Sandbox secret instead (sandbox credentials are user_good / pass_good).

Step 3 of 3 — Enter your credentials
These are saved to data/.env on your machine. They never leave your computer.
Production is free for up to 10 connected accounts and uses real bank data. Sandbox is great for testing — no real banks needed.
✓
You're all set!
Plaid is configured. Now connect your first bank account to start tracking your net worth.
